Meatmeat, An Instagram to Database Connector

Automated message management for Instagram Business accounts.

About This App

This REST application provides automated responses to messages received to a connected Instagram Business account (a.k.a. the bot). The bot helps owners and approved users manage the inquiries and conversions database from the comfort of Instagram.

How It Works

  • Users send a message to the connected Instagram account.
  • e.g.: "list inquiries 1w"
  • The app receives the message via the Instagram Graph API.
  • The system processes the message and returns an automated response.
  • e.g.: "This week: 8 inquiries | 1 conversion | 7 pending"

The app does not interact with users outside of automated messaging. Nor can it interact with users that aren't whitelisted in the dashboard.

Data Usage

The application processes Instagram user IDs, usernames, and message content solely to generate automated responses. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.

Please review our Privacy Policy for full details.
